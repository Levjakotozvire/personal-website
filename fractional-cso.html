The case for fractional

Senior leadership without the overhead

Not every company is at the stage where a full-time CSO makes sense — but every growing company needs someone at the table who thinks strategically about revenue, partnerships, and market position. A fractional CSO gives you exactly that: senior commercial thinking, embedded in your team on a part-time basis.

I've built and led commercial functions at GLAMI Group, FLEXDOG, and Httpool/Aleph — across multiple markets and verticals. I know what good looks like at different stages of growth, and I know how to get a team moving in the right direction quickly. I don't just advise from the sidelines; I work alongside you, in the meetings that matter, on the deals that count.

What I do

What's included

  • Commercial strategy development and alignment with business goals
  • Sales and BD leadership — process design, team coaching, pipeline management
  • Participation in leadership meetings, board prep, and investor conversations
  • Partnership strategy and high-value deal support
  • Market expansion planning — CEE, Balkans, Baltics, and beyond
  • KPI framework, reporting structure, and revenue forecasting
  • Flexible engagement — scale time up or down based on what you need
