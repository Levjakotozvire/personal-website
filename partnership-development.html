Partnerships that last

Most companies approach partnerships the wrong way — treating them as a one-time transaction rather than a long-term commercial relationship. A deal signed is not a partnership built. The real value comes from alignment, activation, and sustained mutual benefit. Getting there requires patience, the right commercial structure, and someone who knows how to navigate complexity on both sides of the table.

My background spans e-commerce, AdTech, SaaS, and retail — including partnership work with TikTok, Snapchat, Amazon, and 100+ brand partners across CEE and beyond. I've led end-to-end business development from first contact to signed agreement to long-term account success. I know what it takes to close deals that matter and to keep them alive once they're live.
